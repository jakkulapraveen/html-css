INTRODUTUION

The unity Game Designer Playbook aims to inspire and instruct game designers who want to learn how to prototype, craft, and test gameplay in Unity. Whether you study game design, are starting out in your career, or have years of professional experience, you can reference this guide as you begin to add Unity skills to your profile. Unity is the most widely used game development platform, so gaining Unity skills can open up new opportunities for you, even if you work on a team using another engine. The contributors to this guide are game designers both within Unity and from our community. The content reflects their vibrant creativity, advanced expertise, and unique mix of skills and interests.
